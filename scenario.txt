🏆 Dashboard 6: Winner Prediction

Purpose: The main feature of your project.

User selects:

Red Fighter
Blue Fighter

The app:

Compares both fighters.
Calculates scores using your rule-based logic.
Displays the predicted winner's name.
Explains why that fighter was predicted.

Example:

Red Fighter : Max Holloway

Blue Fighter : Dustin Poirier

-----------------------

Prediction Score

Max Holloway : 9

Dustin Poirier : 6

-----------------------

🏆 Predicted Winner

🥇 Max Holloway

Reason

✔ Better reach
✔ Better striking accuracy
✔ Better win streak
✔ Younger fighter
